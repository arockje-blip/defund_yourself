COMMANDER'S BRIEFING (TUTORIAL)

Welcome, Commander. You are tasked with defending against the Global Coalition. Here is your strategic guide:

  • Phase 1: Build Your Power. Use the Arsenal & Manpower panels to mobilize Infantry, Navy, and Airforce. Each unit adds to your total Power.
  • Phase 2: Munitions. Visit the Munitions Depot to craft Brahmos Defense missiles (auto-interception) and Attack Missiles (strategic strikes).
  • Phase 3: Radar & Scanning. Click BUILD RADAR to install the 500km long-range array. This is essential for early detection.
  • Phase 4: Attack & Defund. Once you reach 70% of the enemy's power, engage in war. Toggling AUTO ATTACK will use your missiles automatically.
PRO TIPS:
� Focus on Radar early to avoid surprise breaches.
� Combine Defense Brahmos with Attack Missiles for a dual-strike defense.
� Negotiate Alliances to boost your power exponentially without spending resources.
