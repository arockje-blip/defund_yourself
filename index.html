COMMANDER'S TACTICAL BRIEFING

?? THE HUD (TOP LEFT)

RESOURCES: Generating currency via START FARMING. Use this to build units.

OUR POWER: Your total defense strength. Must exceed Coalition Power to survive!

SPACE REMAINING: Troop capacity. Air/Navy units take space, while Infantry is unlimited.

?? COALITION (TOP RIGHT)

ENEMY POWER: Displays USA, UK, PAK, and CHINA strength. Defeat them to level up.

IMPRESSION: Your global reputation. Higher impressions increase Alliance chance.

?? COMMAND CONSOLE (BOTTOM CONTROLS)

  • ?? START FARMING: Main income! Click this to generate resources over time.
  • ?? INFANTRY: Fast, bulk manpower. Doesn't count toward troop limits.
  • ?? SHIPYARD & HANGAR: Build advanced Navy/Air units with custom stealth and EW stats.
  • ?? MUNITIONS: Craft Brahmos (Auto-Defense) and Attack Missiles.
  • ?? RADAR: Essential for early detection of coalition ships and missiles.
  • ?? SECRET OPS: High-risk Cyber/Nuke protocols for extreme situations.

?? HOW TO DEFEND

  1. Spam START FARMING early to max out your bank.
  2. Invest in Navy/Air/Missiles to raise your Power.
  3. The ATTACK AND DEFUND button unlocks at 70% of Coalition Power.
  4. During War, use AUTO ATTACK to handle incoming threats automatically.
